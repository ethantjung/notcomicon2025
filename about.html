Comic‑Con 2025 is an unofficial, student‑produced celebration of all things pop‑culture— from golden‑age comics to cutting‑edge anime. Expect three days packed with autograph sessions, gaming tournaments, interactive panels, cosplay contests, exclusive merch hand‑outs, and nonstop fandom fun at the George R. Brown Convention Center right in the heart of Houston.

Whether you’re a seasoned cosplayer or a first‑time con‑goer, join thousands of fans for an unforgettable weekend of comics, cosplay, and chaos!
